## üé® Design Philosophy

### Principles
1. **Content First**: Good content beats fancy design
2. **Mobile First**: Majority of users are on mobile
3. **Performance Matters**: Fast load times are crucial
4. **Accessibility**: Everyone should be able to use the site
5. **Simplicity**: Clean, clear, easy to navigate

### Visual Direction
- Professional and clean
- Modern but not trendy
- Readable and accessible
- Fast and lightweight
- Personal but professional

## üîë Success Criteria

The portfolio will be considered successful when it:

1. **Looks Professional**: Clean design, no broken elements
2. **Works on Mobile**: Perfect experience on all devices
3. **Loads Fast**: < 3 seconds on 3G connection
4. **Showcases Work**: At least 5 quality projects displayed
5. **Drives Engagement**: Visitors spend > 2 minutes on site
6. **Ranks Well**: Appears in top results for personal name
7. **Generates Opportunities**: Receives inquiries from recruiters/collaborators
