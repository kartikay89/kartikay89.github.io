## üí° Tips for Success

### Do's ‚úÖ
- **Start small**: Pick ONE task and complete it fully
- **Test frequently**: Check your changes in the browser often
- **Mobile first**: Always test on mobile devices
- **Iterate**: It's okay to improve things over time
- **Get feedback**: Share with friends and colleagues

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- **Don't try to do everything at once**: Focus on one category at a time
- **Don't aim for perfection**: Done is better than perfect
- **Don't skip mobile testing**: Most visitors will be on mobile
- **Don't forget accessibility**: Alt text and semantic HTML matter
- **Don't neglect content**: Great content beats fancy design
